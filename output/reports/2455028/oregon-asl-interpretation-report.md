## 5. Market Analysis

- **Industry size and trends:** NAICS 541930 (Translation & Interpretation Services). Strong tailwinds:
  - ADA Title III and Section 504 enforcement (institutional buyers must provide qualified interpreters).
  - Healthcare interpretation demand growing with Medicaid/Medicare reimbursement frameworks.
  - Court interpretation mandates under federal Court Interpreters Act and state equivalents.
- **Growth drivers:** Expanding K-12 IEP/504 interpretation demand; post-COVID rebound in in-person interpretation needs; growth in VRI as a complement (not a substitute).
- **Growth barriers:** RID (Registry of Interpreters for the Deaf) certified interpreter scarcity nationally; price compression from large VRI platforms (LanguageLine, Sorenson) bidding on K-12 / healthcare contracts.
- **Competitive landscape (Oregon-specific):** Multiple regional Oregon ASL agencies plus national VRI providers competing for institutional contracts. Specific peer benchmarks not researched in this pass — flag for follow-up if pursued.
