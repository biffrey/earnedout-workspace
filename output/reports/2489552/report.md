  - Only 7 FT employees

## Risk Factors

- **Valuation:** 4.76x multiple exceeds 4.0x ceiling. Must negotiate from $17M to ~$14.3M (4.0x) as a deal condition.
- **Oil price cyclicality:** Bakken revenue is production-tied; commodity price swings directly impact utilization rates.
- **Geographic concentration:** Single-basin exposure in Williston/Bakken. No diversification.
- **Customer concentration:** Not disclosed — major E&P operators could represent >20% of revenue.
- **Workforce:** 7 FT employees; below 10 threshold but growth path exists.
