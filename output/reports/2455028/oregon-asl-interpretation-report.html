  • Consulting: Communication-access advisory — advising clients when in-person interpreting is required vs. when VRI is acceptable under accessibility standards.

  • Differentiators: 32-year history; reputation for balanced advocacy; bench of ~40 ICs in the Oregon market.

5. Market Analysis

  • Industry: NAICS 541930 — Translation & Interpretation Services. Strong tailwinds from ADA Title III and Section 504 enforcement, healthcare interpretation demand (Medicaid/Medicare), and federal Court Interpreters Act mandates.
  • Growth drivers: Expanding K-12 IEP/504 interpretation; post-COVID in-person rebound; VRI as a complement.
  • Headwinds: RID-certified interpreter scarcity drives wage inflation; price compression from national VRI platforms (Sorenson, LanguageLine) bidding on K-12 and healthcare contracts.
  • Competitive landscape: Regional Oregon ASL agencies + national VRI providers competing for institutional contracts. Specific peer benchmarking deferred — flag for follow-up if pursued.
