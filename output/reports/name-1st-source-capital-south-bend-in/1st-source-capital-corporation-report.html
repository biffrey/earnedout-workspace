Industry size and trends: SBICs are SBA-licensed private investment funds that provide debt and/or equity financing to small businesses using SBA government-guaranteed leverage. The SBIC program has seen renewed federal emphasis (2023–2024 SBA program expansions). Licenses are scarce and tightly regulated under 13 CFR Part 107.

Drivers & barriers: Drivers — SBA leverage amplifies investable capital; program tailwinds from recent modernization. Barriers — the regulatory regime, capital minimums (Regulatory Capital ≥ $5M for post-1996 licensees), and SBA approval of any control change.
