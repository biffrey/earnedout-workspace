⚠️ SBIC Closing Condition — SBA Prior Approval of Change of Control

Acquiring a licensed SBIC requires SBA prior approval of the change of control. Any transaction that transfers control of 1st Source Capital Corporation (the entity holding the SBA SBIC license) cannot close until the U.S. Small Business Administration has approved the change of control. This is a mandatory regulatory closing condition on every SBIC acquisition and must be built into the deal timeline and the purchase agreement (SBA approval as a condition precedent to closing). Source: off-market LeadPacket government fact; SBA SBIC program rules (13 CFR Part 107).
